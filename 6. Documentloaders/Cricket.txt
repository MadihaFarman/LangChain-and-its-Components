🏏 Cricket — The Heartbeat of the Ground 🌥️

On a field of green where dreams take flight,
A bat meets ball in the golden light.
Crowds hold breath as the bowler runs—
A quiet calm… then thunder comes.

The leather spins, the willow sings,
Fielders chase like birds with wings.
Every stroke a story told,
Every wicket a moment bold.

A captain’s mind, sharp as the breeze,
Sets his field with subtle ease.
Under pressure, heroes rise—
Stars are born before our eyes.

From gully shouts to mid-off cheers,
From childhood streets to stadium spheres…
Cricket lives in every beat—
A sport, a passion, a memory sweet.

So let the game forever play,
In sunny skies or clouds of grey.
For cricket isn’t just a game—
It’s heart, it’s hope, it’s burning flame. 🏏🔥
